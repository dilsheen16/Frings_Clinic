About-Us

Frings Clinic has been operational in New Delhi for forty-one years. It was established in the year 1980 by Dr. Gustavo Fring and his wife Dr. Sunita Fring. It has issued over eight million prescriptions. It is located in one of the posh areas of South Delhi. Overtime, senior doctors at the Clinic, exposed to hundreds of patients every day, have become highly experienced and are sought after by the patients. The Clinic and the Dr. Gustavo Fring Society has been responsible for several million charitable prescriptions over four decades.

The Clinic maintains the highest standards of treatment from its doctors. The doctors must meet strict proficiency standards in treatment protocols before they begin prescribing for patients. The Clinic and its doctors do not engage in promotion of their activities. The Clinic is continuously evolving. It invests resources for the development, deployment and use of information technology to increase efficiency, quality of care and the patient experience.

Our doctors also visit different countries on the requests of patients. Patients often arrive in New Delhi, with just the name of the Clinic and eventually find their way here. The doctors attempt to help as many patients, as possible. High ethical standards are practiced at the Clinic. Unreasonable cures or improvement are never assured. Blanket assurances of cure are deemed unethical. Patients are free to undergo investigations and other supplementary interventions at centres of their choosing.
